Display 4-digit numbers on a 5643 Dixie LED tube

Post Time: 2014-12-18 22:12:36 Category: Arduino Parts & Projects Sensors & Accessories

Purpose

Display 4-digit numbers on a 5643S/5643B Dixie LED tube

5643 Dixie LED Pin Graph:

there are two types of 5643 LED pin graph. If one graph is not working, please try the other graph.

:

Connection Graph

Parts and Devices:

  • PLC: Arduino(UNO or Mega 2560 or Leonardo or similar arduino compatible PLC)
  • 4-Digit 5643S Nixie LED tube
  • Jumper wires
  • 1K om resistances
  • bread circuit board

Sample Arduino Code

Download 5643s.ino sketch file and load it into arduino.
